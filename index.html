Who Am I?

Madeleine Campbell

Madeleine Campbell has always had a passion for writing, fashion, and working with an editorial team. Here she has translated these passions into a portfolio website to display her writing and photography. She is currently living in New York City and attending The New School for Journalism and Fashion Studies. Madeleine's main passion is applying intersectional activism to her work and writing articles surrounding people who are often unheard and unseen.

She is an editorial fellow at Motivos Magazine, a bilingual youth publication based out of Philadelphia. You can reach her at janzm956@newschool.edu or @madilonglegs24 on Instagram. More writing examples and portfolio work can be found at her blog, madilonglegs.com.
